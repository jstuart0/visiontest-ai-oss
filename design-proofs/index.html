Five aesthetic proofs, each a standalone HTML file showing a landing hero and a real sample test-result. Every proof has a light/dark toggle in the top-right. Click a card to open full-screen.

Proof 01

Darkroom — warm amber

Analog darkroom/film-editing aesthetic. Film grain, sprocket frames, safelight breathe, phosphor green for pass. Fraunces italic over Fragment Mono.

feel: patient · forensic · warm · analog
Proof 02

Editorial — New Yorker tech

Longform magazine spread. Drop caps, pull-quotes, columns, hairline rules, bone paper, single cadmium-red accent. Fraunces + Newsreader + IBM Plex Mono.

feel: refined · literary · measured · timeless
Proof 03

Observatory — hi-vis brutalist

Monumental condensed typography, hazard-tape rules, thick black borders with offset-yellow shadows, construction orange. Unbounded + Archivo + Space Mono.

feel: bold · industrial · confident · loud
Proof 04

Lab Notebook — field journal

Graph paper, red margin rule, hand-written brand in Caveat, corrections in red pen, caution-yellow flags. Caveat + Source Serif + Inter Tight + Courier Prime.

feel: scientific · hand-crafted · intimate · quirky
Proof 05

Cinema — title-card noir

Letterboxed cinematic title cards, Italiana roman caps + Bodoni italic, teal highlights on ink, celluloid amber for metadata. Takes and shots, not steps.

feel: cinematic · dramatic · refined · quiet
