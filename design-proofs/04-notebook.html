Most testing tools assume you'd rather write code than write words. This one reverses the assumption. You describe a journey — log in, open the settings, verify the banner is gone — and the tool turns it into an experiment, complete with a hypothesis (the goal) and a log of what the browser did, in order, to the millisecond.

↳ “every step is evidence,
every goal is a hypothesis.” — margin note, left side
